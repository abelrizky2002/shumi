# Dota 2: Optimization & Competitive Notes 

### Performance Tuning (GTX 1650)
I spent considerable time benchmarking Dota 2 on my current setup. 
- **The Challenge**: Maintaining stable FPS during chaotic 5v5 team fights with heavy particle effects.
- **My Solution**: I found that using specific launch options (`-novid -high -map dota`) and sticking to DirectX 11 provides a much smoother frame time graph compared to Vulkan on my GTX 1650.
- **Result**: Consistent 120-144 FPS in lane, dropping only slightly during heavy ultimate rotations.

### Meta & Roster Observations
I don't just play; I track how team ownership and regional shuffles (especially in SEA) impact the competitive landscape. It’s interesting to see how organizational stability correlates directly with a team's execution on the map.
